Collaborative Projects

Watts

(2018-2022, 4 years)

Developed Green, flexible anti-microbial, and anti-biofilm hygiene coatings for water and wastewater distribution systems.

Badger Meter

(2018-2022, 4 years)

Critically evaluated the NSF 61 standard and assessed coating performance on reflectors.

Foxconn

(2019, 1 year)

Developed an efficient ion exchange composite material to remove the tetra-methylammonium hydroxide (TMAH) pollutant from wastewater and enable the recycling of adsorbed TMAH molecules through desorption and regeneration processes.
Evaluated activated carbon and commercial ion exchange performance.
Determined the percentage removal, uptake capacity, and breakthrough kinetics of TMAH.

Badger Mining

(2018 – 2019, 1 year)

Developed geo polymers for the removal of heavy metals, caffeine, and organic pollutants from water. Monitored stability testing, conducted statistical analysis to evaluate performance, and operated standard laboratory equipment.
Worked on US-FDA Nanotechnology Fact Sheet, US-FDA Cosmetics Nanotechnology, EPA -Toxic Substances Control Act.
