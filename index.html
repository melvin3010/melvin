Dr.Melvin Samuel

Scientist-Analytical Chemist-Toxicologist-Environmentalist

"Senior Research Analyst at Marquette University with 15+ years in toxicology, formulation, analytical chemistry and

Learn More

Dr. Melvin Samuel, PhD, earned his doctorate in Environmental Science and Engineering with a specialization in Material Science in 2016 from India.
His extensive research experience at institutions such as Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur and University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee has cultivated expertise in analytical instrumentation, water treatment, toxicology and formulation.
Driven by a passion for innovation, he is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of knowledge in his field.

Experience

• Developed coatings for preventing viral, fungal, and microbial contamination.
• Conducted chemical formulations, blending, surface chemistry, and product characterization to ensure quality and performance.
• Proficient in various preparation, purification, separation, and detection methods, including SPE, GC, HPLC, IC, UV, ICP, MS , Particle size analyser and AA.
• Presented research findings through reports, presentations, conferences, and publications.
• Worked on scaling up water treatment technologies for perfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) destruction.

Toxicology and formulation

• Familiar with international consumer product regulations such as CPSC, EPA FIFRA, US FDA, and/or EU BPR regulations.
• Familiar with the General Framework for Assessing the Safety of Nanomaterials in Cosmetic Products, Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FDC Act).
• Worked on pesticide products (p-nitrophenol, 2,4-dichlorophenoxyacetic acid and butachlor), cosmetic and personal care products.
• Perform chemical testing. Toxicology. Consumer product safety.
• Worked on product-related toxicology issues and knowledge in the area of human health risk assessments associated with pesticide products, cosmetic and personal care products.
• Evaluated in vitro (HeLa and A549 cell lines) and in vivo studies (Rat model). Participated in safety- related claims.

Analytical chemistry and Product development

• Expert in performing TLC, LC-MS. GC-MS, UV Vis spectroscopy, AAS, IR, UV DRS, TGA and DLS.
• Conducted chemical performance and tests, review procedures, protocols, quality assurance specifications, test reports and support product development activities.
• Proficient in experimental design, formulation, and applications work for household cleaners.
• Maintained the R&D tracker for marketing submissions, product formula evaluation, lab batching schedule, stability, and safety testing.
• Conducted routine maintenance and troubleshooting of laboratory equipment.
• Followed cGMPs, GLPs, and SOPs, maintaining high quality and safety standards.
• Demonstrated expertise in formulation and applications work, successfully contributing to project plans and meeting critical performance parameters.

Water treatment technologies for PFAS removal

• Investigated PFAS adsorption onto activated carbon.
• Evaluated removal of PFAS during pyrolysis treatments of biosolids.
• Determined Physical-separation and degradation processes of PFAS.
• Collaborated cross-functionally to create project plans, timelines, and risk assessments, contributing to the successful achievement of enterprise goals.
• Worked on destruction of PFAS in AFFF-impacted fire training pit water.
• Knowledge about behaviour and fate of PFAS in drinking water treatment.
• Validation of UV/persulfate as a PFAS treatment of industrial wastewater.
• Developed electrochemical-based approaches for the treatment of PFAS.
• Worked on Concentrate-and-Destroy technology for enhanced removal and destruction of PFAS.
